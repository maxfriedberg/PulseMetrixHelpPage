Privacy Policy – PulseMetrix

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

PulseMetrix collects personal health data from your Apple Health app using HealthKit, including:

  • Heart rate data
  • Workout duration and type
  • Age and gender (entered by the user)

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Display your heart rate zones and exercise summaries
  • Compare your activity against AHA guidelines

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your health data is read from HealthKit and stored only on your device. PulseMetrix does not transmit or back up your health information to external servers.

4. Your Rights

You may revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in your device settings, which will stop PulseMetrix from accessing your data. You can also delete the app to remove any local data.
